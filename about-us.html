CBCRF is a non-profit initiative for improving diagnostics and treatment options for breast cancer that will enhance survival and improve quality of life in every way. CBCRF is a new model for the breast cancer which aims to be a self-sustainable non-profit institution bringing the world class breast cancer care to all India. The hospital is committed to research and dedicated to going beyond state of art facility by pioneering news ways to diagnose, treat and prevent breast cancer.

The Proposed facility includes :

  • Preventive oncology
  • Research
  • Breast screening and diagnostic services
  • Breast Pathology and molecular oncology
  • Breast cancer genetics
  • Breast cancer surgical services.
  • Breast cosmetic and re-constructive services
  • Medical oncology services
  • Radiation oncology services
  • Psycho oncology services
  • Rehabilitation services
  • Palliative care
  • Holistic care- alternative therapies, accessories – prosthesis, hair care & life style mgmt.
  • Provision for long distance patients to stay on / off campus.

MM is a conglomeration of people with different levels of madness. This medley of about 100 "odd" folks enjoy working out together and will go to any extent to spread the joy of fitness and togetherness. Started by a group of friends casually, this group has come a long steady way and have strived to take the road less travelled / biked / ran / trekked.

Fuelled by their passion to create awareness on making fitness as a way of life, they organize “newbie runs/rides” which would witness each Mustang introducing their friends to the addictive world of running or/and cycling.

Madras Mustangs has also organised two major cycling events in

  • Wheel It With Mustangs (WIWM) 2016 and
  • Pedal to Plant with Mustangs (P2P) 2017

Both were 100 km endurance rides that saw close to 200 cyclists being pampered as they pedalled the distance. P2P event also involved planting close to 150 saplings to restore greener environment in our city, after the devastating effect of Cyclone Vardah.

Through Give a Brick Run, they are bringing together the fabulous running community of Chennai for yet another noble cause... to aid the construction of CHENNAI BREAST CANCER RESEARCH FOUNDATION (CBCRF), a new model for breast cancer care that is designed to be a one stop centre of excellence. Here the emphasis will be on improving diagnostics and treatment options for breast cancer that will enhance survival and improve quality of life in every way.

Join us, Run for ‘Give a Brick Run’. For all you know, "Her life starts at your finish line."

How do you become a Mustang?

Most welcome! The only prerequisite so to speak is to have absolute passion and dedication and to spread the importance of fitness with a lot of fun thrown in as well. All you need is the intent to get and stay fit. Though you can find folks with different levels of fitness in this group, the one striking cord you can’t miss is the camaraderie with which this group operates. The MMs watch out for each other and will take all possible steps to ensure that no one is left behind literally.

The personal goals may vary from one Mustang to another...but one can be rest assured that no stone is left unturned to help each one realise their dream.

Come...Become a Mustang....Walk the extra mile the Mustang way !
