Why ‘Give a Brick Run’?

"Her lifet starts at your finish line."

Penn Nalam, a unit of Sri Dhanvantri Trust aims to save women from cancer in time through its mission of educating, screening, diagnosing, treating and counseling. And it is already at it through various means.

CHENNAI BREAST CANCER RESEARCH FOUNDATION (CBCRF), a new model for breast cancer care is a not for profit initiative and will be a one stop centre of excellence where the emphasis will be on improving diagnostics and treatment options for breast cancer that will enhance survival and improve quality of life in every way.

The trust has a committed group of doctors and other team members led by Dr. Venkat Ramakrishnan, a renowned Breast Reconstructive Surgeon and Dr. Selvi Radhakrishna, a renowned Oncoplastic Breast Surgeon. They have a 21,000 sq. ft. land at a premium locality in Anna Nagar. What the trust now needs to realize their dream project are funds to construct., equip and operate the facility to provide state-of-the-art breast cancer care to the women of our country.

Madras Mustangs take pleasure to extend support to this noble cause. There was a no ‘How?’ though. It has to be with the help of the fabulous running community of Chennai. The CBCRF building is a million bricks and let’s contribute as many as we could. So join us, Run to ‘Give a Brick’. For all you know,

"Her life starts at your finish line".
